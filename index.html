Overview

Ariadne is an AI assistant that communicates with a Cloudflare Worker to perform workflow and logic operations.

Information Sent

When you use Ariadne, requests that require external Actions may be sent to the Cloudflare Worker. These requests may include the text you intentionally provide.

Data Usage

Submitted information is used solely to fulfill the requested Action. Data is not sold or used for advertising.

Storage

Some requests may be processed or temporarily stored for operational purposes depending on the configured backend services.

Third Parties

Requests are processed using OpenAI's ChatGPT platform and Cloudflare infrastructure.

Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect transmitted information.

Your Choice

Do not submit confidential or sensitive information unless you understand how the service operates.

Contact
