Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Ariadne is a custom GPT that may use external Actions to communicate with a Cloudflare Worker.

Information Sent

When users interact with Ariadne, text they intentionally provide may be sent to the configured external Action endpoint for processing.

Use of Information

Information is used only to process the requested Action. It is not sold, rented, or used for advertising.

Storage

Depending on the backend configuration, requests may be temporarily processed or logged for operational, debugging, and security purposes.

Third-Party Services

Ariadne may rely on OpenAI services, ChatGPT, Cloudflare Workers, and related Cloudflare infrastructure.

User Responsibility

Users should not submit confidential, sensitive, or regulated personal information unless they understand how the Action works.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the project owner through the associated GitHub account or repository.
